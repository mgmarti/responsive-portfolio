About Me

Hi there!

Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Monica.

I have an educational background in digital media: print and packaging design and in integrated communications, but my professional background is secondary education. I worked with PreK for four years before moving up to teach sixth grade.

I was introduced to web development during the early years of my college career, but ended up pursuing print and packaging design. I've recently decided to further explore the fascinating world of web development and I'm hooked.

Besides spending my time in front of a computer screen lesson planning or learning to write code, I enjoy spending time outdoors. I currently enjoy visiting national parks when I have the time. One of my favorites is Zion National Park in Utah. I also consider watching movies one of my hobbies.
